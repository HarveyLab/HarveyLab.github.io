Bio

Alan completed his PhD in Michael Do's laboratory at Boston Children's Hospital where he studied the biophysical properties of melanopsin-expressing retinal ganglion cells. Now as a joint postdoctoral fellow in the Harvey and Ginty laboratories, he is focused on tracking down the origins of signals in primary somatosensory cortex.
