Anna received her bachelor’s degree in bioengineering at Stanford University, where she worked in Karl Deisseroth’s lab on adapting CLARITY for RNA visualization and neural circuits underlying top-down memory retrieval. She is currently a PhD candidate in the Program in Neuroscience at Harvard Medical School and is interested in using optogenetics to probe computations in visual cortex.Bio
