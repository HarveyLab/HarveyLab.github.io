Bio

Cindy received her B.S. from Johns Hopkins University double majoring in Neuroscience and Cognitive Science with a minor in Applied Maths and Statistics. As an undergraduate researcher, Cindy worked in Jonathan Flombaum’s lab studying human visuospatial perception, and in Marshall Hussain Shuler’s lab to develop behavioral paradigms investigating the mechanism of temporal decision-making in mice. As a graduate student, Cindy is interested in studying the neural mechanism of cognitive behavior.
