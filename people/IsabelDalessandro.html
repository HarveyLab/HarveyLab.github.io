Bio

Isabel received her bachelor’s degree in Neuroscience from Wellesley College. After graduating, she worked as a research assistant in Dr. Rachel Wilson’s lab studying the neural mechanisms of navigation in Drosophila. Isabel is currently a graduate student in the Program in Neuroscience, co-advised by Drs. Chris Harvey and Bob Datta.
