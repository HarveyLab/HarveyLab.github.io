Bio

Lily received her bachelor’s degree in Computation and Neural Systems from Caltech. She studied the stability of place cells in Carlos Lois’ lab. She also studied the function of the hippocampus in auditory learning in Thanos Siapas’ lab. As a graduate student in the Program in Neuroscience, she is interested in studying mice’s ability to solve tasks using prior knowledge.
