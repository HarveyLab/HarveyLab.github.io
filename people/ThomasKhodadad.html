Bio

Thomas received his bachelor’s degree in neuroscience from Middlebury College. His undergraduate research conducted in Dr. Mark Spritzer’s lab focused on the effects of androgens on spatial memory and brain-derived neurotrophic factor. As a research assistant, Thomas is excited to study the function of cell types in the cortex as they relate to spatial navigation.
